DragonStrike Board Game

About Us

Welcome to DragonStrike, a board game that has captured the hearts of adventurers and fantasy enthusiasts for decades.

Our journey began in 1987 when a group of passionate game designers set out to create an unforgettable gaming experience. Drawing inspiration from ancient myths and tales of legendary dragons, they crafted a world of fantasy and magic.

Over the years, DragonStrike has evolved, becoming a beloved classic that has brought joy and excitement to countless players. We take pride in crafting each detail of the game, from the beautifully illustrated cards to the intricately designed game board.

Our mission is to spark the spirit of adventure in every player, transporting them to a realm where they can embark on epic quests, challenge fearsome dragons, and forge alliances with fellow adventurers.

At DragonStrike, we believe that gaming is not just a pastime; it's a way to connect with friends, explore your imagination, and create lasting memories.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. Together, let's continue to discover the magic of DragonStrike and embark on extraordinary adventures.
